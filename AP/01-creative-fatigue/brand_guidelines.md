# Thrive Market Brand Guidelines — Creative Team Reference

## Brand Voice

### Tone: Warm, trustworthy, mission-driven but not preachy

We sound like a knowledgeable friend who happens to know a lot about healthy food and sustainable living. We're enthusiastic but never pushy. We educate without lecturing. We inspire without guilt-tripping.

**We are:**
- Warm and approachable — like talking to a friend at the farmers market
- Confident in our mission — we know we're doing good
- Practical and helpful — we solve real problems
- Inclusive — healthy living is for everyone, not just the privileged

**We are NOT:**
- Preachy or moralistic — never "you should" or "you need to"
- Clinical or cold — no medical-journal tone
- Exclusive or elitist — never "finally, groceries for people who care"
- Aggressive or fear-based — no scare tactics about food

### Voice Examples

| Instead of... | We say... |
|---|---|
| "Stop poisoning your family with processed food" | "Give your family cleaner ingredients without the markup" |
| "Only smart shoppers join Thrive" | "Members save an average of $32 per order on the brands they love" |
| "The cheap stuff is killing you" | "Premium ingredients at prices that work for real budgets" |
| "You can't afford NOT to eat organic" | "Organic doesn't have to mean expensive" |

## Visual Standards

### Photography
- **Bright, natural lighting** — never dark or moody
- **Real food imagery** — actual products, actual meals, no overly styled food
- **Clean backgrounds** — white, light wood, marble, bright kitchen settings
- **People look natural** — diverse, genuine smiles, not model-perfect poses
- **Packaging is visible but not dominant** — the food is the hero

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Thrive Green (#2E7D32), Warm White (#FAFAFA)
- **Secondary:** Earth Brown (#5D4037), Sky Blue (#42A5F5)
- **Accent:** Sunshine Yellow (#FFD54F) — used sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- **Avoid:** Neon colors, pure black backgrounds, dark/moody palettes

### Typography
- Headlines: Clean sans-serif, bold weight
- Body: Readable, warm, slightly rounded sans-serif
- Never: Script fonts, all-caps body text, decorative fonts

## Hard Don'ts

1. **No fear-based messaging.** Never use health scares, disease warnings, or "toxic" language to sell products. We attract through aspiration, not anxiety.

2. **No "cheap" language.** We're affordable, not cheap. Words like "bargain," "dirt cheap," "rock bottom" undermine the premium positioning. Use: "value," "savings," "affordable," "smart pricing."

3. **Always lead with member benefit.** Every ad should make clear what the MEMBER gets. Not what Thrive is — what Thrive does for YOU.

4. **No competitor bashing.** We can compare prices factually (e.g., "25-50% less than traditional retail") but never name competitors negatively. Neutral comparison is fine; attacks are not.

5. **No unsubstantiated health claims.** We don't cure, treat, or prevent anything. We provide clean, high-quality food. Let the ingredients speak.

6. **No exclusionary language.** Never imply that healthy eating is only for certain income levels, lifestyles, or demographics. The membership model exists to make this accessible.

7. **No urgency manipulation.** Limited-time offers are fine; false scarcity is not. "Today only!" when it's available every day = trust violation.

8. **No stock photography.** All imagery should be original, UGC, or custom-shot. Generic stock photos undermine authenticity.

## CTA Guidelines

### Preferred CTAs
- "Start saving" (value angle)
- "Join the mission" (sustainability angle)
- "Try Thrive free" (trial offer)
- "See what's inside" (curiosity)
- "Get your first box" (tangible)

### Avoid
- "Buy now" (too transactional)
- "Don't miss out" (false urgency)
- "Sign up" (too generic)
- "Act fast" (pressure tactic)

## Platform-Specific Notes (Meta)

### Feed (Static & Carousel)
- 1:1 or 4:5 aspect ratio
- Text overlay: max 20% of image area
- First image must stop the scroll — no slow builds
- Carousel: tell a story across slides, don't repeat the same message

### Reels / Video
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- Hook in first 3 seconds — the scroll is ruthless
- Captions required (80%+ watch muted)
- 15-30 seconds optimal; 60 seconds max
- End with clear CTA and branded end card

### UGC
- Must feel authentic — don't over-produce
- Creator should mention Thrive by name within first 5 seconds
- Disclosure: "Paid partnership" label required
- Creator's genuine experience > scripted testimonial
