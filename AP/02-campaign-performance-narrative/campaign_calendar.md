### 4. Lapsed Member Win-Back Email Series
- **Channels:** Email only
- **Launched:** April 7 (Monday)
- **Audience:** Members who haven't ordered in 60-90 days (segment size: ~28,000)
- **Sequence:**
  - Email 1 (Mon): "We miss you" + personalized product recommendations based on last 3 orders
  - Email 2 (Wed): Social proof — "Here's what other members are loving right now" + trending products
  - Email 3 (Fri): Incentive — free shipping on next order + $10 credit expiring in 7 days
- **Notes:** This is a new 3-touch sequence replacing our previous single "win-back" email. Hypothesis: graduated escalation from emotional to incentive will outperform leading with discount
