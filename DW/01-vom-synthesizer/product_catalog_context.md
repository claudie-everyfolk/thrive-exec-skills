# Product & System Changes — Last 30 Days

## Frozen Category Expansion (Launched: March 18, 2026)
- Added 47 new frozen SKUs including frozen meals, ice cream, and frozen produce
- New cold-chain partner (ColdPack Logistics) handling fulfillment for West Coast orders
- East Coast orders still fulfilled through existing partner (FreshShip)
- Known issue: ColdPack has had 3 reported incidents of insufficient dry ice in shipments during Week 1
- Frozen items now represent 12% of total SKUs (up from 7%)

## New Promo Code System (Launched: April 1, 2026)
- Migrated from legacy promo engine to new rules-based system
- Supports stacking: member discount + promo code (previously either/or)
- New codes launched with migration: SPRING25 (25% off first order), FREESHIP (free shipping on $49+), THRIVEMORE (15% off frozen category)
- Known issue: THRIVEMORE code throws "invalid code" error for ~8% of members; engineering investigating — appears related to members whose accounts were created before 2024
- Auto-apply feature for FREESHIP was delayed; currently requires manual entry

## Membership Renewal Email Redesign (Launched: March 25, 2026)
- New email template with clearer renewal date, pricing, and cancellation instructions
- Added a "manage membership" CTA button linking to account settings
- Unintended change: renewal reminder now sends 3 days before charge (previously 7 days)
- Annual members receive one email; monthly members receive email each cycle
- Open rate improved 22% vs. old template; click-through up 15%

## Other Changes
- **Website checkout flow**: Minor UI update on April 5 — "Place Order" button moved from right side to center. No known issues reported by engineering.
- **Sierra AI update**: Knowledge base expanded from 158 to 165 articles on March 28. New articles cover frozen category FAQs and updated return policy for perishables.
- **BPO Bogota ramp**: 8 new agents started March 20, completed training April 3. Now handling live chat and email.
