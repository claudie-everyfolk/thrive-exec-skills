## Theme 1: Late Delivery Frustration (Shipping)
**Tickets:** 148 | **Avg CSAT:** 3.6 | **Trend:** Stable

Members continue to report deliveries arriving 2-3 days past the estimated window. Highest concentration in Southeast region (FL, GA, TX). FreshShip's on-time rate dipped to 91% in March (target: 95%).

**Representative quotes:**
- "My order was supposed to arrive Tuesday and it's now Friday. The tracking hasn't updated in 3 days."
- "This is the second month in a row my delivery was late. I'm paying for a membership and can't rely on the shipping."

**Recommended action:** Ops to review FreshShip SLA compliance for Southeast region. Consider backup carrier for high-volume zip codes.

---

## Theme 2: Membership Auto-Renewal Confusion (Membership/Billing)
**Tickets:** 89 | **Avg CSAT:** 3.2 | **Trend:** Up +18% vs. Feb

Members report being surprised by auto-renewal charges. Common pattern: member forgot they signed up, didn't see renewal notice, or couldn't find cancellation option.

**Representative quotes:**
- "I was charged $60 and I don't even remember signing up for an annual membership."
- "Where do I cancel? I've been clicking around for 10 minutes."

**Recommended action:** Marketing to review renewal reminder cadence and clarity. Product to add cancellation link to member dashboard homepage.

---

## Theme 3: Damaged Items in Transit (Product Quality/Shipping)
**Tickets:** 74 | **Avg CSAT:** 3.8 | **Trend:** Stable

Broken glass jars, crushed boxes, and dented cans. Correlates with larger orders (6+ items). Packaging appears adequate for smaller orders but insufficient for heavier shipments.

**Representative quotes:**
- "My olive oil bottle was shattered inside the box. Everything was covered in oil."
- "Three cans were badly dented. I don't feel safe eating from dented cans."

**Recommended action:** Ops to review packaging guidelines for orders exceeding 15 lbs. Consider box-in-box for glass items.

---

## Theme 4: Promo Code Issues (Billing)
**Tickets:** 52 | **Avg CSAT:** 3.9 | **Trend:** Up +8% vs. Feb

Members report promo codes not applying at checkout. Most common: expired codes, codes not valid for their membership tier, and stacking errors.

**Representative quotes:**
- "The code WELCOME20 says it's expired but I just got the email today."
- "I tried applying two codes and the site just removed both of them."

**Recommended action:** Engineering to audit promo code expiration logic. Marketing to add clearer terms to promo emails.

---

## Theme 5: Return Process Friction (Returns)
**Tickets:** 68 | **Avg CSAT:** 3.5 | **Trend:** Stable

Members find the return/refund process unclear — especially for perishable items where a return isn't practical. Common question: "Do I need to ship it back?"

**Representative quotes:**
- "The yogurt arrived warm. Do you really want me to mail back warm yogurt?"
- "I submitted a return request 5 days ago and haven't heard back."

**Recommended action:** Product to surface "no-return refund" policy more prominently for perishables. Member Services to reduce refund approval SLA from 72h to 48h.

---

## Top 5 Actionable Findings

1. **Late deliveries in Southeast** — 148 tickets, CSAT 3.6. FreshShip SLA miss. → Ops
2. **Auto-renewal surprise charges** — 89 tickets, up 18%. Renewal notice inadequate. → Marketing + Product
3. **Damaged items in heavy orders** — 74 tickets. Packaging gap for 15+ lb orders. → Ops
4. **Return process confusion for perishables** — 68 tickets. Policy not visible. → Product
5. **Promo code failures** — 52 tickets, up 8%. Expiration logic bug suspected. → Engineering
