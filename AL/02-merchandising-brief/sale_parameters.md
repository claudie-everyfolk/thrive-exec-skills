# Summer Wellness Sale -- Campaign Parameters

## Campaign Overview

- **Sale Name:** Summer Wellness Sale
- **Dates:** June 9-13, 2026 (Monday through Friday)
- **Duration:** 5 days
- **Format:** Daily email + homepage takeover + curated collection pages
- **Participating Brands:** 80
- **Target:** All Thrive Market members (2.1M active)

## Daily Touchpoints

Each day includes:
- **Morning email** (6:00 AM PT) -- hero imagery, daily theme, featured products, member savings callout
- **Homepage takeover** -- refreshed daily with theme-specific hero banner, category carousels, and editorial content
- **Collection page** -- dedicated sale landing page organized by daily theme
- **Push notification** (10:00 AM PT) -- single product spotlight with savings percentage
- **Social posts** -- 2 Instagram stories + 1 feed post per day aligned with daily theme

## Merchandising Principles

1. **Lead with value on Day 1.** The opening day sets the tone. Feature the deepest discounts and most recognizable brands to drive early traffic and establish sale momentum. Day 1 should have the broadest appeal -- don't niche down until Day 2+.

2. **Front-load new brands.** Our members are discovery-driven. New-to-Thrive brands should appear prominently in Days 1-3 to maximize exposure during peak traffic. By Day 4-5, traffic naturally declines, so save proven performers for those days.

3. **Balance categories across days.** No single day should be all snacks or all supplements. Each day needs representation across food, beverages, household, and personal care. Aim for at least 4 distinct categories per day.

4. **GLP-1 and clean eating are trending.** Products that align with GLP-1 medication dietary guidance (high protein, low sugar, portion-controlled, whole foods) should be explicitly called out. This is our fastest-growing member segment. Similarly, "clean eating" and "ingredient transparency" resonate strongly right now.

5. **Highlight member favorites strategically.** Don't cluster all member favorites on one day. Spread them across the sale to maintain daily engagement. Each day should have at least 2-3 member favorites as anchors.

6. **Margin-aware programming.** Days with high-visibility placement (Day 1, Day 3 mid-week peak) should include a healthy mix of High margin tier brands. Don't burn the best margin brands on low-traffic days.

7. **Tell a story across 5 days.** The sale should feel curated, not random. Each day's theme should connect to the next -- there should be a narrative arc from Day 1 (broad wellness) to Day 5 (specific lifestyle).

## Key Trends to Leverage

- **GLP-1 compatible products** -- high protein, low sugar, portion awareness. 34% of our members report being on or considering GLP-1 medications
- **Clean beauty crossover** -- members who buy clean food increasingly want clean personal care. Cross-category merchandising opportunity
- **Functional beverages** -- kombucha, adaptogenic drinks, collagen waters growing 28% YoY on platform
- **Fresh & frozen expansion** -- Thrive recently expanded cold chain. Fresh and frozen deserve prominent placement to drive trial
- **Family wellness** -- baby/kids products see highest engagement when merchandised alongside parent-focused wellness, not isolated

## Discount Structure

- Standard sale discount: 25% off
- Member favorites: additional 5% (30% total)
- New-to-Thrive brands: introductory 35% off first purchase
- Flash deals: 1 per day, 40% off for first 500 orders (use for high-margin hero products)

## Creative Direction

- **Photography style:** Bright, natural light. Lifestyle > product-on-white. Show products in use -- kitchen counters, meal prep, morning routines.
- **Color palette:** Summer greens, warm golds, clean whites. Avoid neon or overly clinical.
- **Copy tone:** Warm but smart. "Your summer wellness starts here" not "HUGE SAVINGS!!!" We're a trusted curator, not a discount warehouse.
- **Member voice:** Incorporate 1-2 member reviews or testimonials per day.
