# Thrive Market Ingredient Quality Standards

*Version 4.2 -- Last updated March 2026*

## Overview

Every product sold on Thrive Market must meet our ingredient quality standards. These standards reflect our commitment to clean, transparent, and responsibly sourced products. Our members trust us to do the vetting they don't have time to do themselves.

All products are classified into one of four categories during review:
- **APPROVED** -- All ingredients acceptable, no documentation gaps
- **CONDITIONAL APPROVAL** -- Contains Restricted ingredients; approval pending required documentation
- **REJECTED** -- Contains one or more Unacceptable ingredients
- **NEEDS REVIEW** -- Contains Unknown ingredients requiring FSQA team evaluation

---

## Unacceptable Ingredients (Automatic Rejection)

The following ingredients result in automatic product rejection. No exceptions.

### Artificial Sweeteners
- Sucralose (Splenda)
- Aspartame
- Acesulfame potassium (Ace-K)
- Saccharin
- Neotame
- Advantame

### Artificial Preservatives
- TBHQ (tertiary butylhydroquinone)
- BHA (butylated hydroxyanisole)
- BHT (butylated hydroxytoluene)
- Sodium benzoate (when paired with ascorbic acid / vitamin C)
- Potassium bromate

### Artificial Colors
- Any FD&C color (Red 40, Yellow 5, Yellow 6, Blue 1, etc.)
- Caramel color (Class III and IV -- contains 4-MEI)

### Artificial Flavors
- Any ingredient listed as "artificial flavors" or "artificial flavoring"
- Artificial vanilla flavor / vanillin (synthetic)

### Hydrogenated Oils
- Partially hydrogenated oils (any source -- soybean, cottonseed, palm, etc.)
- Fully hydrogenated oils

### Sweeteners
- High fructose corn syrup (HFCS)
- Corn syrup solids (when used as primary sweetener)

### Preservatives
- Sodium nitrite / sodium nitrate (in processed meats)
- Potassium sorbate (in beverages and dairy -- acceptable in topical/beauty products only)

### Other
- MSG (monosodium glutamate) when added as a standalone ingredient
- Carrageenan (degraded and undegraded)
- Propylene glycol
- Titanium dioxide

## Restricted Ingredients (Requires Documentation)

These ingredients are permitted but require specific documentation from the vendor before approval.

### Palm Oil and Palm-Derived Ingredients
- **Requirement:** RSPO (Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil) certification -- Mass Balance or higher
- **Documentation:** Current RSPO certificate with supply chain model indicated
- **Applies to:** Palm oil, palm kernel oil, palm fruit oil, any palm-derived emulsifier

### Soy and Soy-Derived Ingredients
- **Requirement:** Non-GMO Project Verified OR USDA Organic certification
- **Documentation:** Non-GMO Project Verification letter or organic certificate
- **Applies to:** Soy lecithin, soy protein isolate, soybean oil, soy sauce, tofu, tempeh, edamame

### Corn and Corn-Derived Ingredients
- **Requirement:** Non-GMO Project Verified OR USDA Organic certification
- **Documentation:** Non-GMO Project Verification letter or organic certificate
- **Applies to:** Corn starch, corn flour, maltodextrin, dextrose, corn syrup (non-HFCS, non-primary sweetener use)

### Canola Oil
- **Requirement:** Non-GMO Project Verified OR organic certification AND expeller-pressed or cold-pressed
- **Documentation:** Non-GMO verification + processing method confirmation
- **Applies to:** All canola oil

### Natural Flavors
- **Requirement:** Vendor must provide a natural flavor declaration confirming: (a) derived from the named food source, (b) no synthetic solvents used in extraction, (c) no artificial preservatives in the flavor system
- **Documentation:** Natural flavor declaration letter from flavor supplier
- **Applies to:** Any ingredient listed as "natural flavors" or "natural flavor"

### Stevia
- **Requirement:** Whole-leaf or minimally processed extract preferred. Reb-A isolates acceptable with documentation of processing method.
- **Documentation:** Processing method declaration
- **Applies to:** Stevia leaf extract, Reb-A, steviol glycosides

### Silicon Dioxide (Anti-Caking Agent)
- **Requirement:** Must be used at less than 2% of product weight. Vendor must confirm percentage.
- **Documentation:** Percentage confirmation letter
- **Applies to:** Silicon dioxide in any product

## Non-GMO Requirements

- All products containing corn, soy, canola, sugar beet, alfalfa, papaya, summer squash, or cotton-derived ingredients must provide Non-GMO Project Verification OR USDA Organic certification
- Products making "Non-GMO" claims on packaging must have third-party verification (Non-GMO Project Verified preferred)
- If a product is USDA Organic certified, non-GMO verification is automatically satisfied (organic standards prohibit GMOs)

---

## Sustainable Sourcing Requirements

### Palm Oil
- RSPO certification required (Mass Balance minimum, Identity Preserved or Segregated preferred)
- Products using palm oil without RSPO certification are automatically placed in Conditional Approval

### Cocoa and Chocolate
- Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance, or Direct Trade certification preferred
- Not required for approval but flagged for member transparency

### Seafood
- MSC (Marine Stewardship Council) or ASC (Aquaculture Stewardship Council) certification required
- Wild-caught: must not be on Monterey Bay Aquarium Seafood Watch "Avoid" list

### Coffee and Tea
- Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance, or Direct Trade certification preferred
- Not required for approval but flagged for member transparency

---

## Category-Specific Rules

### Meat and Poultry
- No added hormones (required for all beef; USDA prohibits in poultry/pork, but must be stated)
- No antibiotics (raised without antibiotics / no antibiotics ever)
- Sodium nitrite/nitrate prohibited -- celery powder or cultured celery extract acceptable as alternative curing agent
- Animal welfare certification preferred (Global Animal Partnership, Certified Humane, Animal Welfare Approved)

### Dairy and Dairy Alternatives
- rBGH/rBST-free required for all dairy products
- Carrageenan prohibited in all dairy and dairy alternative products
- Organic preferred but not required

### Baby and Kids Products
- Zero tolerance for any Unacceptable ingredient -- no exceptions, no documentation path
- Additional restricted: added sugars limited to less than 3g per serving
- All ingredients must be recognizable as whole foods or minimally processed

### Supplements and Vitamins
- Third-party tested (USP, NSF, ConsumerLab, or equivalent)
- No proprietary blends without full ingredient disclosure
- cGMP facility certification required

## Yeast Extract Policy

Yeast extract is classified as a **borderline ingredient** at Thrive Market:
- **Permitted** in savory products (broths, sauces, crackers, seasonings) where it serves as a flavor enhancer
- **Flagged for FSQA review** when used in products marketed as "clean label" or "no added MSG" (since yeast extract contains free glutamates)
- When flagged, FSQA determines if the usage is consistent with our clean label positioning

## Review Process

1. **Initial Screen:** All ingredients checked against Unacceptable list. Any match = automatic rejection.
2. **Restricted Check:** Remaining products checked for Restricted ingredients. Any match = Conditional Approval pending documentation.
3. **Non-GMO Check:** Products with at-risk crops checked for verification. Missing verification = Conditional Approval.
4. **Category Rules:** Category-specific rules applied. Violations may result in rejection or conditional approval.
5. **Unknown Ingredients:** Any ingredient not appearing in Acceptable, Restricted, or Unacceptable lists is flagged as Unknown and escalated to FSQA.
6. **Final Determination:** FSQA reviews escalations within 5 business days.
