## Key Observations

1. **Sierra dominates spend** — $700K is 54% of core AI spend. It's also delivering the highest measurable impact (68% of member contacts automated). The question: is the ROI strong enough to justify the expansion to 88%?

2. **Claude costs are accelerating** — Started at $50K/yr for Engineering. Now trending $25-30K/mo as non-engineering teams adopt it. Projected annualized: $310K. Meanwhile, Gemini is free and some teams are switching.

3. **Novi replacement is a clear win** — $175K/yr contract ending, replaced by in-house AI. Pure savings.

4. **Tool proliferation is real** — Marketing alone uses 17 AI tools. Across the company, we count 40+ distinct AI tools or integrations. No central vetting process exists.

5. **Compliance tooling is an investment, not a cost** — Vanta ($92K) and DocuSign IAM ($84K) prevent audit failures that would cost 10-100x more. SOX non-compliance risk is real.

6. **Missing: centralized AI budget** — Each function manages its own AI spend. No single owner. No company-wide optimization.
