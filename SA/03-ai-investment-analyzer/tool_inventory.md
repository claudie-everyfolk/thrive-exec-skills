## Notes

- **Claude Enterprise** contract renews in Q3. Token costs have been trending up ~15% month-over-month as adoption grows. Current run rate: ~$1,500/month in tokens. 200 seats were purchased based on full engineering org + some non-eng expansion. Actual active usage is significantly lower.
- **GitHub Copilot** has the broadest deployment (500 seats) but usage telemetry is limited. GitHub's dashboard shows "acceptance rate" but not hours saved or productivity impact. Some teams report it's essential; others say they rarely use it.
- **New Relic** is the largest single line item. Contract was negotiated 4 years ago and is up for renewal in Q4. The SRE team considers it indispensable. However, New Relic AI features overlap with what Claude can do (natural language querying, alert analysis, incident summarization). Open question: could we reduce New Relic scope and use Claude for some analysis workflows?
- **CodeRabbit** trial ends in 6 weeks. Initial feedback is mixed — some teams love it, others find it noisy. Need to decide: expand, keep at current pilot, or cut.
- **Sierra** handles 40% of tier-1 support tickets autonomously. Member satisfaction scores for AI-handled tickets are 4.2/5 vs 4.5/5 for human-handled.
- **Coveo** contract renewal coming in Q1 2027. Product team exploring whether Snowflake Cortex + Claude could replace it.
- **Fountain** has reduced warehouse hiring time from 14 days to 5 days. Clear ROI for high-volume seasonal hiring.
