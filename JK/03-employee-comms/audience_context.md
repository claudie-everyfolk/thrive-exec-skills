## Fulfillment Center Details

### Batesville, IN

- **Union status:** UFCW Local 700 represents ~180 hourly workers (pick/pack roles in Zone A). Remainder are non-union.
- **Demographics:** Average age 34; 45% female, 55% male; 28% Spanish-speaking (Spanish is primary language for ~12%)
- **Turnover:** ~35% annual (industry average for FC roles)
- **Shift structure:** 3 rotating shifts; all-hands meetings are impractical — information must reach all shifts through posted communications and manager cascades
- **Tech access:** Thrivers have personal phones but no company-issued devices; break room TVs display company announcements; physical bulletin boards in break areas and entry vestibules
- **Communication cadence:** Weekly manager huddles (15 min, start of each shift); monthly site town hall (video recorded for off-shift viewing)

### Los Angeles, CA

- **Union status:** Non-union
- **Demographics:** Average age 31; 52% female, 48% male; 22% Spanish-speaking
- **Turnover:** ~30% annual
- **Shift structure:** 2 shifts; quarterly all-hands in person
- **Tech access:** Same as Batesville — personal phones, break room TVs, bulletin boards
- **Communication cadence:** Weekly manager huddles; quarterly site town hall

## Communication Channels — Reach & Timing

| Channel | Reaches | Best For | Lead Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **All-company email** | Everyone (but FC workers may not read for 24-48 hrs) | Official announcements, policy changes, detailed information | Send 24hr before Slack |
| **Slack #general** | HQ + Remote (immediate); FC (variable) | Quick updates, celebrations, links to detailed docs | Same day as or day after email |
| **Manager cascade** | Everyone via their direct manager | Sensitive changes, anything requiring 1:1 conversation | Brief managers 48-72 hrs before all-company comms |
| **FC break room TVs** | FC hourly workers | Announcements, enrollment reminders, celebration content | Update slides 24hr before email |
| **FC bulletin boards** | FC hourly workers | Printed summaries, QR codes to enrollment pages, posters | Post 24hr before email |
| **All-hands meeting** | HQ + Remote (live); FC (recorded) | Major announcements, CEO messages, Q&A | Monthly cadence; special sessions for major changes |
| **Benefits wiki** | Self-service for anyone with intranet access | Detailed FAQ, enrollment instructions, comparison charts | Update before announcement goes out |
| **Manager talking points** | People managers only | Guidance for handling questions in 1:1s and team meetings | Distribute 48-72 hrs before all-company comms |

## Sequencing Best Practice

For sensitive announcements, use this sequence:

1. **Day -3:** Brief senior leadership and get final sign-off
2. **Day -2:** Distribute manager talking points; hold manager Q&A session
3. **Day -1:** Update FC displays and bulletin boards; update benefits wiki/FAQ
4. **Day 0 (morning):** Send all-company email
5. **Day 0 (afternoon):** Post in Slack #general with link to email/FAQ
6. **Day +1 to +5:** Manager 1:1 conversations with directly affected Thrivers
7. **Day +7:** Hold info sessions / town halls for Q&A

For positive announcements, sequence can be compressed:
1. **Day 0:** Simultaneous email + Slack + FC displays
2. **Day 0:** Manager cascade (share, celebrate, answer questions)
3. **Day +1:** Social media coordination (external)

## Language Considerations

- **Spanish translations** required for all critical communications at Batesville FC (benefits changes, policy changes, safety information)
- **Reading level:** Target 8th-grade reading level for all-company communications. FC communications should target 6th-grade level.
- **Accessibility:** All digital communications must be screen-reader compatible. Video content must have captions.
