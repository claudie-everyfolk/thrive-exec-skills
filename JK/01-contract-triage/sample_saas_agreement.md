## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 **"Authorized Users"** means Customer's employees, contractors, and agents who are authorized by Customer to access the Platform.

1.2 **"Customer Data"** means all data, content, and information uploaded to, processed by, or generated through Customer's use of the Platform, including without limitation member purchase histories, behavioral analytics, marketing campaign data, audience segmentation profiles, and derived insights.

1.3 **"Platform"** means Prism Analytics' proprietary marketing analytics and customer intelligence platform, including all dashboards, APIs, data connectors, and reporting tools.

1.4 **"Subscription Term"** has the meaning set forth in Section 8.1.

1.5 **"Professional Services"** means implementation, training, custom integration, and consulting services provided by Provider as described in one or more Statements of Work.

---

## 2. SUBSCRIPTION AND ACCESS

2.1 **Grant of Access.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Platform during the Subscription Term for Customer's internal business purposes.

2.2 **User Limits.** Customer's subscription includes up to seventy-five (75) Authorized Users. Additional users may be added at Provider's then-current per-user rate.

2.3 **Usage Restrictions.** Customer shall not: (a) sublicense, sell, or lease access to the Platform; (b) reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any portion of the Platform; (c) use the Platform to process data on behalf of third parties; (d) remove or modify any proprietary notices on the Platform.

---

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

3.1 **Subscription Fees.** Customer shall pay Provider an annual subscription fee of One Hundred Seventy-Five Thousand Dollars ($175,000.00), payable in advance within thirty (30) days of each annual billing date.

3.2 **Professional Services Fees.** Professional Services shall be billed at Provider's standard rates as set forth in the applicable Statement of Work. Current rates are $350/hour for implementation and $425/hour for custom development.

3.3 **Price Escalation.** Provider reserves the right to increase subscription fees by up to eight percent (8%) upon each renewal, with written notice provided no less than sixty (60) days prior to the renewal date.

3.4 **Late Payment.** Overdue amounts shall accrue interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law.

3.5 **Taxes.** All fees are exclusive of taxes. Customer is responsible for all sales, use, and similar taxes arising from this Agreement.

## 4. DATA RIGHTS AND PRIVACY

4.1 **Customer Data Ownership.** Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to Customer Data.

4.2 **Provider Data License.** Customer hereby grants Provider a worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, and create derivative works from Customer Data in aggregated and anonymized form for the purposes of (a) improving the Platform, (b) developing new products and features, (c) benchmarking and analytics, and (d) marketing Provider's services. Provider may retain aggregated data following termination of this Agreement.

4.3 **Data Processing.** Provider shall process Customer Data in accordance with its Privacy Policy, available at prismanalytics.com/privacy, as updated from time to time. Provider processes data in the United States, with backup processing in Ireland and Singapore. Provider will notify Customer of material changes to data processing locations with sixty (60) days' prior written notice.

4.4 **Data Security.** Provider shall maintain commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect Customer Data. Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II certification, which shall be provided to Customer upon request.

4.5 **Data Subject Requests.** Provider shall assist Customer in responding to data subject access, deletion, and opt-out requests to the extent such requests relate to Customer Data processed through the Platform. Response time for Provider assistance shall not exceed fifteen (15) business days.

4.6 **Subprocessors.** Provider engages third-party subprocessors to assist in delivering the Platform. A current list of subprocessors is available at prismanalytics.com/subprocessors. Provider will notify Customer of new subprocessors by updating such list no less than thirty (30) days before engagement. Customer's sole remedy for objection to a new subprocessor is termination of this Agreement.

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 **Subscription Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of three (3) years (the "Initial Term"). Thereafter, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods (each a "Renewal Term") unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

8.2 **Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement upon sixty (60) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within the sixty (60) day notice period.

8.3 **Termination for Convenience.** Provider may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon one hundred eighty (180) days' written notice to Customer. In the event of such termination, Provider shall refund any prepaid fees attributable to the unused portion of the then-current Subscription Term.

8.4 **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination or expiration: (a) Customer's access to the Platform shall immediately cease; (b) each party shall return or destroy the other party's Confidential Information; (c) Provider shall make Customer Data available for download in a standard format for sixty (60) days following termination, after which Provider may delete Customer Data; (d) Sections 4.2, 5, 7, 9, and 10 shall survive termination.
